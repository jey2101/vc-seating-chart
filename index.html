How to Use the VC Seating Chart App

Setting Up a Class

  • Click Manage Classes to create a new class. Give it a name and set the number of rows and columns to match your classroom layout.
  • Use the dropdown in the toolbar to switch between classes.

Adding Students

  • Click Import Students and paste or type your student names — one per line, or from a spreadsheet.
  • Students will appear in the Unassigned list on the right.

Arranging Seats

  • Drag a student from the Unassigned list and drop them into any empty seat.
  • Drag a seated student to a different seat, or back to the Unassigned list to unseat them.
  • Right-click any seat to mark it as unavailable (e.g. a broken desk), or to re-enable it.

Printing

  • Click Export PDF to print a seating chart. Select a class and page orientation.
  • Each seat includes a small grid of boxes for tracking attendance or participation.

Backing Up Your Data

  • Click Backup to save all your classes to a file on your computer. Keep this somewhere safe, like your Google Drive.
  • Click Import Backup to restore your data from a previously saved backup file.
  • Your data is only stored in this browser on this device — backing up regularly protects your work.
